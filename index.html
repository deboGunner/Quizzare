QUIZZARE 2k17

Quizzare is an intra-college Quiz competition hosted under by MCKVIE under the CSI Student Chapter Activities. Quizzare promises to test the inquizzitiveness of the contenstants with some brainstorming quizzing sessions. After the success of Quizzare 2k16, this year promises to be more enlighting, illuminating and fun. The event is going to take place on 7th April, at MCKVIE campus. We would also like to thank our sponsors in advance for sponsoring this event. So hope you all turn up for MCKVIE's mega quiz show. Till then "Keep Calm and Happy Quizzing"
